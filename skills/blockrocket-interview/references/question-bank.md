# Question bank

Organised by theme. Check `plans/04-interview-questions.md` for what's already answered before asking. Add new questions here as they occur; mark retired ones rather than deleting.

## Origin (2015–2018)

- How did you two actually meet? First conversation you remember — where, what about?
- First Blockchain Manchester meetup: venue, turnout, what went wrong?
- The moment each of you decided Ethereum was worth betting years on?
- Why the name "BlockRocket"? Rejected names you can admit to?
- Who was the first person to pay BlockRocket actual money, and for what?

## KnownOrigin (2018–2022)

- The basement launch: whose basement, and one detail nobody would guess?
- First sale that made it feel real — artwork, price, where were you?
- The school-bus years (2019–20): what kept you shipping when nobody was buying?
- The 2021 boom: the single most surreal day?
- The eBay approach — how did first contact happen? (NDA-safe version)

## The misses (the best content — handle warmly)

- The CryptoPunk zombie from Erick (Snowfro/Art Blocks) offered as payment and declined — when, for what work, why did you say no, what's it worth now, and how often does it come up in the pub? **Priority: this is a flagged story moment.**
- Other payments-in-tokens you turned down (or accepted!) — any that went the other way?
- A project you said no to that you shouldn't have? One you said yes to that you regret?

## eBay era & return (2022–2024)

- One thing building inside a Fortune 500 taught you that consultancy clients now benefit from?
- The day the layoffs hit — one line you're happy to publish.
- Coming back to BlockRocket: relief, fear, or both?

## Now & next (rolling — refresh per session)

- x402 / agent payments: what specifically excites you, what have you tried, what would you build with it tomorrow?
- What's on your workbench right now that nobody knows about?
- Which frontier topic do clients ask about that you think is overrated? Underrated?
- Who's the most interesting person who turned up at Frontier Fridays lately?

## Character & mission

- One human detail each for the bios (the thing colleagues tease you about).
- A time you advised a client NOT to use blockchain.
- "The difference between us in 2017 and us now is ______."
- "Building innovative things with curious partners in frontier tech" — would you say that in the pub? Write the pub version.
