From Community to Core Engineering

BlockRocket didn't start in a boardroom; it started in the trenches of the Manchester tech scene. Our story is rooted in the belief that decentralisation is the most significant architectural shift of our generation. We don't just advise on Web3—we lived its evolution.

What began as a bedroom project evolved into KnownOrigin, a platform that sparked a global crypto-art movement. We navigated the complexities of scaling a decentralised ecosystem, raised venture capital, and ultimately led the platform through its acquisition by eBay.

Following the acquisition, Andy and James transitioned to shaping global Web3 strategy at the highest level. However, the mission remained clear: returning to their first love of working directly with innovators.

Today, BlockRocket applies those "battle-tested" insights to help you navigate a landscape where we have already seen—and built—what comes next.
