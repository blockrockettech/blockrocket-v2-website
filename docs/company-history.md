## Timeline

### 2015–2017 — Community Roots

Andy Gray and James Morgan met and began collaborating through the nascent Manchester blockchain community. They co-created **Blockchain Manchester**, a grassroots developer meetup that brought together engineers, founders, and enthusiasts from across the North of England. This community would later seed multiple Web3 companies and projects.

### 2018 — BlockRocket and KnownOrigin Founded

BlockRocket was incorporated in 2018 as a Web3 engineering and advisory consultancy. In the same year, Andy Gray, James Morgan, and **David Moore** launched **KnownOrigin** — a decentralised NFT art marketplace built on Ethereum.

KnownOrigin launched with 10 artists and operated from a Manchester basement. It was one of the earliest Ethereum-native NFT marketplaces, predating the mainstream NFT boom.

### 2020 — EIP-2981 (NFT Royalty Standard)

James Morgan co-authored **EIP-2981**, the Ethereum Improvement Proposal that defined a standard on-chain interface for NFT royalty payments. This proposal became a foundational piece of NFT infrastructure, adopted across OpenSea, Foundation, and most major NFT platforms. It was formally included in Ethereum's standards process and presented by James at ETHDenver 2022.

### 2022 — KnownOrigin Acquired by eBay

In June 2022, eBay acquired KnownOrigin in an undisclosed deal. By the time of acquisition, KnownOrigin had facilitated approximately $7.8M in total trade volume. All three founders — Andy Gray, James Morgan, and David Moore — joined eBay as part of the deal.

The acquisition was notable as one of the first major e-commerce company acquisitions of an NFT marketplace and reflected eBay's push into digital collectibles.

### 2023–2024 — Return to Independence

Following eBay's February 2024 layoffs (approximately 30% of staff), the KnownOrigin marketplace was shut down in July 2024. Andy and James returned to BlockRocket, refocusing the consultancy with the depth of experience gained from building, scaling, and exiting KnownOrigin.

### 2024–Present — BlockRocket v2

BlockRocket operates as a veteran Web3 consultancy. Services include:

- **Strategy & Advisory** — helping enterprises and startups navigate decentralised systems
- **Smart Contract Engineering** — Solidity, audits, protocol design
- **DeFi & Self-Custody Onboarding** — education and technical setup for institutions and high-net-worth individuals
- **Education** — workshops, training, and speaking engagements

Andy runs **Frontier Fridays**, a weekly Manchester drop-in event for builders working across AI, Web3, and biotech.
