## Andy Gray

- **LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/andrewmgray/

- **Role:** Co-founder, BlockRocket; Co-founder, KnownOrigin

### Background

Andy Gray is a Manchester-based entrepreneur and full-stack software engineer who co-founded both BlockRocket and KnownOrigin. He is one of the original architects of the Manchester blockchain community, co-creating **Blockchain Manchester** alongside James Morgan — a grassroots meetup group that became a hub for UK Web3 developers and enthusiasts.

He runs **Frontier Fridays**, a weekly drop-in event in Manchester for builders working across AI, Web3, and biotech. The event is supported by Maxwell Bond and Superteam UK and provides a regular meeting point for the Northern tech community.

After the KnownOrigin acquisition by eBay in June 2022, Andy joined eBay as a Director and worked within their NFT/digital collectibles division.

## James Morgan

- **LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/jamesemorgan/

- **Medium:** https://medium.com/@james.morgan
- **Role:** Co-founder, BlockRocket; Co-founder, KnownOrigin; Co-author EIP-2981

### Background

James Morgan is a senior full-stack software engineer with 10+ years of experience and deep Ethereum expertise. He co-founded BlockRocket and KnownOrigin alongside Andy Gray, and is widely known in the Ethereum standards community as the co-author of **EIP-2981** — the NFT Royalty Standard that became a foundational part of how royalties are enforced on-chain.

James presented EIP-2981 at **ETHDenver 2022** and has been a regular speaker and contributor in the Ethereum ecosystem. He was also an organiser of Blockchain Manchester.

After the KnownOrigin acquisition by eBay in June 2022, James joined eBay as part of the NFT team.
